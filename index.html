F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Anything you want to know about us? You can find out the most asked questions here, otherwise you can email at enquiries@hotbeans.com for other questions.

  • What kind of websites can you create?

    Our team can create websites for any purpose, there is nothing we will turn down.

  • Why did you choose the name "Hot Beans"?

    When the company began, our two founders would work day and night completing developments and learning new techniques, coffee was the main source of energy during this time, hence the name "Hot Beans".

  • Who can become a trainee?

    Our trainee program takes anyone from complete beginners to intermediate developers. Anyone is considered to join Hot Beans as a trainee provided they show dedication and drive towards programming and development. All of our trainees are interviewed before joining.
